Global Medication Adherence — Why This Matters

  • Non‑adherence is widespread: WHO estimates only ~50% adherence to long‑term therapies in developed countries.
  • Health impact: Missed or late doses worsen control of chronic diseases (e.g., hypertension, diabetes), increasing complications.
  • Healthcare burden: In the U.S., non‑adherence is associated with ~10% of hospitalizations and substantial avoidable costs annually.
  • Common causes: forgetfulness, complex regimens, side‑effects, low health literacy, and lack of timely prompts.

Sources: World Health Organization (Adherence to Long‑Term Therapies), CDC, peer‑reviewed adherence studies. Actual figures vary by region and condition.

What You Control

  • Choose your alarm sound from curated options.
  • Vibration on or off.
  • Post‑alarm and missed reminder notifications on or off.
  • Language follows your device automatically.

Simple Steps

  1. Add medicine name, type (pill/tablet/syringe/syrup), and time.
  2. At the exact time, the alarm screen appears over the lock screen.
  3. The app speaks the instruction 4 times within the minute.
  4. Confirm Taken or Not Taken. Missed reminders follow your settings.

Privacy & Safety

  • Data stays on your device — no accounts or cloud uploads.
  • Only basic info is stored: medicine names, times, and preferences.
  • MedPing supports adherence but does not replace medical advice.

Permissions & Tips

  • Notifications and alarms: to alert you at the exact time, over the lock screen.
  • Vibration (optional): for tactile feedback if enabled.
  • Allow MedPing to run without strict battery restrictions for best punctuality.
